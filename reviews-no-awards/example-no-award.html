Summary

A cozy café with good pastries but slow service.

Full Diary Entry

I stopped by this café during my Monterey trip. The pastries were warm and flaky, and the atmosphere was peaceful. But the wait time for a simple drink was nearly 20 minutes, and the seating was limited. Still, it had charm, and I’m glad I tried it once.

Tips for Visitors

  • Go early to avoid the rush.
  • Grab a pastry first if the drink line is long.
