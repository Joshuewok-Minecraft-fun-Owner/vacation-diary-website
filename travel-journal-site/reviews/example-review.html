Harbor View Diner

Monterey, CA • March 2026

Award

Cozy Corner Award

Summary

Big windows, bigger pancakes, and staff that feels like regulars even on your first visit.

Full Diary Entry

I found this place on a foggy morning when the harbor was still half asleep. The coffee came out hot, the pancakes were the size of the plate, and the server joked like we had known each other for years.

Tips for Visitors

  • Ask for a window seat.
  • Split the pancakes unless you are starving.
